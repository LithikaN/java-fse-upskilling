Local Community Bulletin

The City Council has announced a series of community events to encourage participation and strengthen neighborhood engagement. Residents are invited to attend the Annual Music Festival scheduled for next Saturday at Central Park. In addition, a Community Food Fair will be held at the Town Hall where local vendors will showcase traditional and modern cuisines. Families can enjoy cultural performances, food stalls, and interactive activities throughout the day. The Sports Development Committee has also planned a Community Sports Meet featuring football, cricket, and athletics competitions. Registration is open for all age groups, and winners will receive certificates and prizes. Environmental awareness programs, including a tree plantation drive and recycling workshop, will take place later this month. Volunteers are encouraged to participate and contribute to a greener and healthier community. Citizens can visit the Local Community Event Portal regularly for updates, announcements, and registration details regarding upcoming activities.
